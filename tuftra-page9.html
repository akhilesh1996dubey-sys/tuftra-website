Who We Are

A Marketplace Built
for Rug Artisans

Tuftra is a curated online marketplace connecting verified artisan rug sellers with buyers across the United States. We specialize in one thing — rugs — and we do it with care, transparency, and a deep respect for the craft.

We noticed that talented rug makers from Pakistan, Turkey, Morocco, and beyond struggled to reach USA buyers directly. Platforms like Amazon took 15–20% commission. Etsy charged listing fees. Wayfair took up to 33%. We thought that was unfair.

So we built Tuftra — a platform where sellers keep what they earn, buyers shop with confidence, and every rug has a story worth telling.

How We Work
Our Platform Model
Tuftra operates as a marketplace — we connect buyers and sellers, hold payments securely, and ensure every transaction is safe and fair for both sides.
1
Verified Sellers Join
Sellers apply and are reviewed by our team. Only genuine artisans and quality rug sellers are approved. Every seller agrees to our terms before listing.
2
Buyers Shop Safely
Buyers browse and purchase with confidence. Payments go to Tuftra first — never directly to the seller until delivery is confirmed. 30-day returns guaranteed.
3
Sellers Ship & Get Paid
Sellers pack and ship orders directly. Once delivery is confirmed, Tuftra releases payment to the seller every 5 days. Transparent, automatic, reliable.
4
We Handle Disputes
If anything goes wrong — wrong item, damaged goods, late delivery — Tuftra mediates fairly. Buyers get refunds. Sellers face proportionate penalties.
5
Everyone Grows
As sellers get more reviews and better scores, they get more visibility. As buyers return, the marketplace grows. We succeed when you succeed.
6
Full Transparency
Every payout includes a detailed breakdown. Every penalty is explained. Every policy is clear and fair. No hidden fees, no surprises — ever.
